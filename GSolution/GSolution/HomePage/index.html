Welcome to
Marine Hub

Life below water is essential to our planet's ecosystem. The ocean covers more than 70%
of the earth's surface and is home to millions of species of marine plants and animals.
It also provides a source of food and livelihood for millions of people around the world. However,
overfishing, pollution, and climate change are threatening the health and well-being of our oceans
and the creatures that live in them. It's important that we take action to protect our oceans and
ensure that they remain healthy and vibrant for generations to come
